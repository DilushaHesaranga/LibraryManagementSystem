FrequentlyAsked Questions

What is GreenBite?

GreenBite is a simple, browser-based toolkit for healthy living—easy recipes, a calorie & nutrition calculator, quick workouts, and mindfulness timers.

Is GreenBite medical advice?

No. Content and tools are for education only. Talk to a qualified professional for medical or dietary advice.

How accurate is the calorie calculator?

It provides estimates of daily energy needs based on your inputs and activity level. Use it as a guide, then adjust based on real-world results.

Which units does the calculator use?

Height in cm and weight in kg. If you use other units, convert before entering your data.

Do you store my personal data?

We’re privacy-first. Some features (newsletter opt-in, mindfulness streaks, etc.) may save small items in your browser’s localStorage. We don’t see that data.

How do I clear saved data or streaks?

Clear site data for GreenBite in your browser settings to reset localStorage (this removes saved emails, preferences, and streaks).

Can I filter recipes (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)?

Yes. Use the category filters or search by name. Each recipe includes ingredients, steps, and a nutrition table.

Where do the nutrition numbers come from?

They’re calculated from ingredient data and standard references. Values can vary with brands and portions—use them as guidance.
